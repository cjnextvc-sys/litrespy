How Litrespy Works

Cheap Fuel in 3 Taps

No sign-ups. No tracking. Just the cheapest petrol near you, live from official government data.

⚖️
01

Reported by Law

QLD & NSW law requires every service station to submit price changes within 30 minutes of updating their boards. The number you see is the number on the bowser.

⚡
02

Aggregated Live

Litrespy collects prices automatically from the official Government FuelCheck feeds and renders them on a live map every 15 minutes. No estimates, no guesses.

🔔
03

Alert When It Drops

Set a price threshold and get notified the moment fuel falls below it near you. Our smart alert system is what makes Litrespy different from every other fuel app.
