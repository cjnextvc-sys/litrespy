💡 Fuel Saving Tips

Save more at the bowser

01

Fill Up on Tuesdays or Wednesdays

Fuel prices in Queensland typically follow a 42-day wholesale cycle. Prices are consistently lowest mid-week — Tuesday and Wednesday are usually your best bet for E10 and Unleaded 91.

02

Use Discount Dockets

Flybuys points at Coles Express and Woolworths Everyday Rewards discounts can save 4–10 cents per litre. Stack these with a low-cycle day for maximum savings.

03

Consider E10

E10 fuel is typically 3–5 cents cheaper than Unleaded 91. Most modern cars (2000 onwards) can run it safely — check your owner's manual or fuel cap.

04

Check Before You Fill

Prices can vary by 20+ cents per litre between stations just a few kilometres apart. Always check Litrespy before you pull in — it takes 30 seconds and can save you $10 a tank.
