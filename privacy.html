Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

Plain English Summary

1. Introduction

Welcome to Litrespy. This Privacy Policy explains how Litrespy ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our website at litrespy.com.au (the "Service").

Litrespy is a free fuel price comparison tool for Queensland and New South Wales drivers. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling any information responsibly.

By using Litrespy, you agree to the practices described in this policy. This policy complies with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Data

✓ Location is only requested when you tap "Near Me". It is used solely to show nearby stations and is never stored or transmitted to our servers.

With your explicit permission, your browser may share your device's location with Litrespy to show fuel stations near you. You can deny or revoke location permission at any time through your browser settings. Litrespy will continue to work — you can search by suburb instead.

2.2 Search Queries

When you search for a suburb or postcode, that query is sent to Nominatim (OpenStreetMap's geocoding service) to find the location. Litrespy itself does not store your search queries.

2.3 What We Do NOT Collect

  • Names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • Passwords or payment information
  • Account or profile information (no accounts required)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Browsing history outside of Litrespy

3. How We Use It

The minimal information that passes through Litrespy is used only to provide the Service:

  • Location — to show petrol stations near you and calculate distances
  • Suburb search — to pan the map to the correct location

We do not use any information for marketing, advertising targeting, profiling, or any purpose other than operating the fuel price comparison tool.

4. Data Sharing

✓ We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to any third party.

Litrespy does not share personal information with third parties, advertisers, or data brokers. The only data that leaves your device is:

  • Your suburb search query — sent to Nominatim/OpenStreetMap to look up coordinates (see Third-Party Services below)
  • Your rough location — sent to the Overpass API to fetch nearby station data, if you use Near Me

We may be required to disclose information if required by Australian law or a valid court order. We have no personal data to disclose beyond the above.

5. Data Storage & Security

Litrespy does not operate its own servers or databases for user data. The website is a client-side application — all fuel map functionality runs in your browser.

Your location, if shared, is used momentarily in your browser's memory to make API requests and is not written to any database or log file controlled by Litrespy.

We use HTTPS throughout the site to encrypt data in transit.

6. Your Rights

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Make a complaint if you believe we have breached the APPs

As Litrespy does not collect or store personal information, there is no personal data held about you to access or correct. If you believe otherwise, please contact us.

You can withdraw location permission at any time in your browser settings (Settings → Site Permissions → Location).

7. Cookies & Tracking

✓ Litrespy uses no analytics, tracking cookies, or advertising scripts.

We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or any analytics platform
  • Facebook Pixel or social media tracking
  • Advertising cookies or retargeting scripts
  • Session cookies for identifying returning users

The only browser storage Litrespy uses is temporary in-memory state (JavaScript variables) to display the map during your session. This is cleared when you close the tab.

8. Third-Party Services

Litrespy uses the following third-party services to operate. Each has its own privacy policy.

OpenStreetMap / Nominatim
Used to convert suburb/postcode searches into map coordinates. Your search query is sent to Nominatim servers. OpenStreetMap's privacy policy applies: osmfoundation.org.

Overpass API
Used to fetch petrol station locations from OpenStreetMap data. Your approximate location is sent to the Overpass API server to retrieve nearby stations. No personal identifiers are sent.

CARTO (Map Tiles)
The map background tiles are served by CARTO. Your IP address may be visible to CARTO's servers as part of standard web requests. CARTO's privacy policy: carto.com/privacy.

Google Fonts
We use the Inter typeface loaded from Google Fonts. Your IP may be logged by Google as part of serving the font file. Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy.

QLD & NSW Government FuelCheck
Fuel price data is sourced from the Queensland Government FuelCheck program and the NSW Government FuelCheck program. No personal data is sent to these services.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or a privacy concern, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@litrespy.com.au
  • General: hello@litrespy.com.au

We will respond to privacy enquiries within 30 days. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Continued use of Litrespy after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We recommend reviewing this page periodically.
