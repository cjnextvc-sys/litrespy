How It Works

Where the data comes from

Station Locations

Every petrol station on the Litrespy map comes from OpenStreetMap (OSM) — a community-maintained open dataset used by millions of apps worldwide.

  • Updated continuously by thousands of Australian volunteers
  • Includes station name, brand, and street address
  • Only real stations — no pins in rivers or oceans
  • 8 km search radius from your location

Fuel Prices

Queensland and NSW both run mandatory fuel price reporting schemes that require every servo to report prices daily.

  • QLD FuelCheck — Queensland Government price feed
  • NSW FuelCheck — NSW Government price feed
  • Prices updated every 15–30 minutes
  • All fuel types: E10, Unleaded 91, P95, P98, Diesel
Our Story

Why Litrespy exists

It started with a simple frustration. Fill up on a Monday in Brisbane and you might pay 175 cents per litre. Drive past the same servo on Wednesday and it's 152 cents. That's a $11.50 difference on a 50-litre tank — just for timing your fill-up right.

Queensland's fuel market has a 42-day price cycle that most drivers don't know exists. Prices climb steadily for weeks, then crash almost overnight. Knowing when you're at the top or bottom of that cycle — and which servo in your area is cheapest right now — can save a Queensland family $400–$600 a year.

Litrespy is built to give every Queensland and NSW driver that advantage. No gimmicks, no premium subscriptions. Just the cheapest fuel near you, right now.
